About Me

This is what I do.

I love writing code since writing my first program in C and making it print "Hello World" for the first time, and manipulating it to produce the desired output. I have been obsessed with the idea of using software to solve practical problems. Programming is a never-ending puzzle that I am passionately engaged in solving. I believe in the power of programming to transform and improve the lives of people around the world.

I learned C, then C++, Searcing on the internet, I could find anything remotely related to software development. I was insatiable.

As I started going deeper into it, more resources were available to me than ever imagined as a beginner. I studied new languages, algorithms, compilers, higher mathematics, all with pretty much the same fascination that drove me as a beginner. And it was here that I learned indeed how much there was yet to learn.

Specialities languages: C, C++, Python

My specialities include quickly learning a new skill and programming language, problem-solving. I'm still enthusiastically grabbing onto any other programming languages, frameworks, or principles I can integrate into the coding in my head.
